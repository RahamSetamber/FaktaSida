Did you know? Meerkats have a unique way of protecting themselves from predators. When a meerkat spots danger, it lets out a high-pitched alarm call to alert the rest of the group. The other meerkats will then stand on their hind legs and look out for potential threats while the first meerkat goes to investigate. This behavior is known as "sentinel behavior" and allows the meerkats to better protect themselves from predators.
